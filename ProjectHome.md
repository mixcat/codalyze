## CyzRgb ##
Pwm rgb 3-led implementation for ATtiny45, an alternative firmware for
[BlinkM](http://thingm.com/products/blinkm)

Includes I2C Master and slave implementations for ATtiny45 (and all other atmel chips
with USI)
